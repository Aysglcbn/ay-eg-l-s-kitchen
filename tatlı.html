Künefe

How is the Turkish style künefe made?

  • Materials
  • How is it done?
  • The most subtle parts of the work

Materials

  • 500 ml of water

  • 400 ml granulated sugar

  • 5 ml of lemon juice

  • 400 g kadayıf

  • 250 g of cunefe cheese

  • 120 g Butter, melted

  • 10 g of butter (to lubricate the pan)

  • 400 g of powdered Pistachios

How is it done?

  1. For the sorbet; mix the water and granulated sugar in a saucepan to make the sugar melt.

  2. When the sugar melts and starts to boil, add the lemon juice.

  3. When it gets January, remove it from the stove and let it rest at room temperature.

  4. For the cake; cut the cake into small pieces with your hand in a bowl.

  5. Add the melted butter to it, adding it little by little, and blend it nicely.

  6. Slice the künefe cheese finely.

  7. Lubricate the pan and spread a portion of the kadayifs on its bottom, pressing it very well with your hands.

  8. Place the sliced cheese on top.

  9. Spread the remaining kadayifs so that they are completely covered over and press them again in the same way.

  10. Turn January the stove. Cook one side on low heat for 5 minutes, turning it over and over.

  11. Close the saucepan lid completely over it and turn the pan over. Transfer the kadayıf to the lid.

  12. Lubricate the pan again. Carefully transfer the dogrose to the pan and fry the other side as well.

  13. Give the warm sorbet to the doghouse. When the sorbet is ready, remove it from the January and transfer it to a plate.

  14. Serve it by decorating it with pistachios

The most subtle parts of the work

  • If you wish, you can prepare your cakes in smaller pans. When you are going to cook in smaller pans, you can turn a plate of the same sizes on it and close the lid

  • You can serve it with cream or ice cream next to it.
